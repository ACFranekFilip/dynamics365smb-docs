# Deleting Posted Invoices and Credit Memos
In Iceland, in accordance with legislation, ADD INCLUDE<!--[!INCLUDE[navnow](../../includes/navnow_md.md)]--> has been modified to prevent you from deleting posted invoices and credit memos. When you attempt to delete one of these documents, a message will note that the posted document may not be deleted. The **Delete** command is not available on the ribbon for the following document types:  
  
-   Sales invoices  
  
-   Sales credit memos  
  
-   Purchase invoices  
  
-   Purchase credit memos
